Our Company Overview

  • TSN Systems, a global IT solutions and services provider based in New York, United States, Though recently established, our company is powered by a team of seasoned professionals with over a decade of proven industry experience. Collectively, we have delivered high value solutions to more than 150 clients including Fortune 500 organizations achieving an exceptional success rate. Our track record spans businesses of all sizes, where we have consistently transformed challenges into measurable results, earning the trust and confidence of our clients.

    The consultants who now form TSN have successfully executed more than 500 complex IT implementation projects and supported clients in integrating emerging technologies. Their integrated, reliable, responsive, and cost-effective approach has consistently delivered results, while their cutting-edge services and deep expertise have positioned them as a seamless extension of client's teams.

    We offer a wide range of technology solutions and services, including Strategy, Applications, Data, Intelligence, Security, Infrastructure, Cloud, DevOps, Digital Transformation, Identity and Access Management, Managed Services, and Technology Advisory.

  • Culture and Diversity

    TSN Systems is a global company with diversity embedded in its culture. We bring together team members from a wide variety of backgrounds. Our team-oriented environment is innovative and fast-paced. At TSN Systems, we value our employees, empower them, recognize, and reward them for their excellent performance. We invest in our employees by providing ample opportunities to learn the latest technologies and acquire new skills and industry knowledge.

    TSN Systems is an equal opportunity employer. We provide equal employment opportunities in our recruitment and selection policies. We also respect differences in perspective as we firmly believe these differences build an innovative and healthy work environment.

  • Our Core Values

    At TSN Systems, our business is guided by a strong set of core values that shape every decision and interaction.

    We expect all our employees to embrace these values, taking ownership and reflecting them in their daily work. Our core values are: Commitment, Respect, Distinction, Innovation, Integrity.
