SAP Transformation Services



SAP Functional Consulting & Configuration

TSN delivers expert consulting and configuration across core SAP functional modules to drive operational excellence:


  • SAP SD (Sales & Distribution): Streamline sales cycles, order management, and revenue processes. SAP FICO (Finance & Costing): Enhance financial reporting, compliance, and accounting workflows. SAP MM (Materials Management): Optimize procurement, inventory control, and supplier collaboration. SAP PP, HR & Others: Align production planning, human resources, and business operations with industry best practices.

Our functional expertise ensures faster adoption, process efficiency, and measurable ROI on SAP investments.


SAP Technical Development with BTP Expertise

We specialize in SAP technical enablement, leveraging the full potential of the SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP):


  • Application Development: Build scalable, user-centric apps using SAP Fiori, UI5, and CAP. System Integration: Seamlessly connect SAP and third-party systems via SAP Integration Suite. Data & Analytics: Unlock insights with SAP Data Warehouse Cloud and SAP Analytics Cloud. AI & Automation: Drive intelligent workflows and predictive capabilities.

TSN empowers businesses to extend SAP landscapes while remaining cloud-ready and innovation-driven.


SAP Master Data Governance (MDG)

Data integrity is central to digital success. TSN’s MDG services ensure:


  • Centralized Governance: Manage master data across Finance, Material, Vendor, and Customer domains. Data Quality Assurance: Implement validation, enrichment, and deduplication processes. Regulatory Compliance: Maintain audit-ready data governance aligned with global standards.

We help clients establish a single source of truth, enabling accurate analytics and confident decision-making.


SAP S/4HANA Conversion & Modernization

TSN guides enterprises through seamless transitions to SAP S/4HANA, unlocking next-gen capabilities:


  • Real-Time Processing: Leverage in-memory computing for faster insights. Simplified Architecture: Adopt lean data models for streamlined operations. Modern UX: Enhance user experience with SAP Fiori. Flexible Approaches: Choose Brownfield or Greenfield strategies tailored to business needs.

Our proven methodologies ensure minimal disruption and maximum transformation value.


SAP Administration & Security (Basis Services)

Our Basis and Security services ensure resilient, secure, and high-performing SAP environments:


  • System Administration: Manage installations, upgrades, performance tuning, and monitoring. Security & Compliance: Design roles, manage access, and support audits. Landscape Management: Enable high availability, disaster recovery, and system refreshes. Cloud Enablement: Support hybrid deployments on SAP RISE, AWS, Azure, or GCP.

TSN safeguards your SAP infrastructure while enabling scalability and compliance.


Continuous Innovation in SAP Post S/4HANA Conversion

After transitioning to SAP S/4HANA, TSN enables organizations to adopt a continuous innovation model that ensures long-term agility and competitiveness:


  • Regular Feature Adoption: Leverage SAP’s bi-annual release cycles to integrate new functionalities and enhancements without disrupting operations. Process Optimization: Continuously refine business processes using embedded analytics, intelligent automation, and real-time insights. Innovation with BTP: Extend capabilities through SAP Business Technology Platform—enabling rapid development, integration, and deployment of new solutions. User Experience Evolution: Enhance usability with evolving SAP Fiori apps and personalized digital workspaces. Governance & Change Management: Implement structured governance to manage innovation cycles, user adoption, and compliance.

TSN ensures that your SAP ecosystem remains future-ready, adaptive, and aligned with evolving business needs.
